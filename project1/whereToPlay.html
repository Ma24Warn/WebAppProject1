Where to Play Video Games

Gaming PC

Computers have been around for many years now, and it is only recently that we have begun dedicated these devices to be the best at running video games. While not necessarily a console, a gaming pc has much more range in all aspects because of it ability to be customized by the player. Gaming pc's can run from very cheap to absurdly expensive, but it should be noted that these prices almost always correlate with the quality of the gaming computer.

Xbox Original

First released on November 15, 2001, the original Xbox was Microsoft's first console that they developed and their entry into the gaming market. This console came into the market as a direct competitor of Sony and Playstation. The original Xbox sold over 24 million units and would be the start of Micorsoft's gaming empire.

Xbox 360

The Xbox 360 was originally released on November 22, 2005 and was Microsofts newest entry in the gaming world. It was the next generation console of the original Xbox and was an even bigger hit than it. The Xbox 360 was also the firt Microsoft console to feature Xbox live, a way for people to play games together over the internet. The Xbox 360 sold over 84 million units and was a massive success.

Xbox One

The Xbox One was released on November 22, 2013 and was the direct successor of the Xbox 360. While no major changes were made to this console, it provided a direct upgrade in technology which allowed people to play the newest games at even higher settings. Microsoft already had a strong foundation at this point, so the Xbox One was another huge success. The Xbox One sold over 58 millions units.

Xbox Series S

The Xbox Series S was released on Novermber 10, 2020 and was Microsoft's next generation of console after the Xbox One. As expected, it was a massive success and further allowed people to play the newest games on even higher settings. The Xbox Series S sold over 8 million units (as of September 20 2021) and being the newest Xbox console, these numbers are still on the rise.

Playstation 1

The Playstation 1 was originally released on December 3, 1994 and was Sony's first entry into the world of gaming. Although originally only released in Japan, it was eventually sold worldwide and quickly became a staple of the gaming industry. The Playstation 1 was a massive success, selling over 102 million units worldwide.

Playstation 2

The Playstation 2 was released on March 4, 2000 and was the next generation console for Sony. After the success of the first console, the Playstation 2 quickly soared to the top and became an icon of the gaming world. As a direct competitor of the Xbox 360, it was a juggernaut in Sony's gaming empire. The Playstation 2 sold over 158 million copies.

Playstation 3

The Playstation 3 was released on Novermber 11, 2006 and was the next console after the Playstation 1 and 2. While the Playstation 2 was a very difficult console to live up to, the Playstation 3 was still a massive success, especially in such a heated competition with other brands such as Microsoft. The Playstation 3 was also a direct competitor of the Xbox 360 and was another solid foundation in Sony's legacy. The Playstation 3 sold over 87 million units by the time is was discontinued.

Playstation 4

The Playstation 4 was released on November 15, 2013 and was the newest in the line of Sony consoles. Released just before the Xbox one, the two consoles were the staples of next gen consoles at the time. The Playstation 4 had a lot to live up to after the massive success of the previous consoles. The Playstation 4 did just that. It sold over 106 million units and became an icon of the newest generation of consoles.

Playstation 5

The Playstation 5 was released on November 19, 2020 and is currently Sony's newest generation console. The Playstation 5 was a massive success and even had a lot of controversy around it regarding the difficulty to purchase one around its release. It is a top of the line console and even has VR support which has been something that most consoles have stayed away from. The Playstation 5 has sold over 30 million units as of January 4, 2023 and is very likely to keep on the rise.

Nintendo Gamecube

The Nintendo GameCube was originally released in Japan on September 14, 2001 and was the direct successor of the Nintendo 64, another famous console. While the GameCube was not Nintendo's first console, it was certainly one of their most well known and iconic consoles of the early 2000s. This console sold over 21 million units, a massive success for its time, especially while having to live up to the name of the Nintendo 64.

Nintendo Wii

The Nintendo Wii was released on November 19, 2006 and was the next in line console from Nintendo after the GameCube. Just like it's predecessors, the Wii became an iconic console that many people around the world fell in love with. The Wii uses a special type of wireless controller that had motion sensing capabilities, a staple feature of the console and a major turning point for wireless and motion detection controllers. The Wii's massive success earned itself a 101 million units sold.

Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch was released on March 3, 2017 and is currently the newest console/gaming device from Nintendo. With the arrival of the Switch came many new groundbreaking features for consoles. The Switch's use of two motion controllers that could be combine together to make a single regular controller was a groundbreaking feature that no other console had done before. To top that off, the Switch was not only a console which you could play games on your TV with, but it was also a mobile gaming device, similar to that of the Nintendo Gameboy and Nintendo DS. As of December 31, 2022 the Switch has sold over 122 million units, and considering it is one of the newest consoles mentioned on this list, it is likely to increase even further beyond that.
